fall summit

2021

The Chemical Society Association fall summit will gather all experts and academics in the filed of chemistry to discuss about the role that chemistry played in the covid-19 drug discovery process and also discuss on the impact of chemistry in a post-covid-19 world. This year, the summit will be partnered by world renown chemical organisations making it a very important one

Please feel free to get in touch with us for further questions about CSA fall sumit 2021!
